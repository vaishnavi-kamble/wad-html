Top Waterfalls to Visit

Dudhsagar Falls, Goa

One of the tallest waterfalls in India, nestled in the Western Ghats.

Jog Falls, Karnataka

A majestic segmented waterfall formed by the Sharavathi river.

Nohkalikai Falls, Meghalaya

India’s tallest plunge waterfall located near Cherrapunji.

How to Reach

Most waterfalls are accessible via local transportation options. Here's how you can reach them:

  • Dudhsagar Falls: Reach by train to Kulem or by jeep safaris from Goa.
  • Jog Falls: Accessible from Shimoga via road or train.
  • Nohkalikai Falls: Nearest airport is Shillong. Taxis and local transport are available.
